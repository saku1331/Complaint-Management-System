OVERVIEW


Complify basically helps the college students to rectify their problems in any regard related to the hostel issues. The Admin helps to keep track of the complaints by updating the status of issues as soon as complaints are resolved. The Wardon ensures the proper fucntioning of all such activities by monitoring the numerious complaints and thier statuses filed by the students. Complify, therefore, assists college students in resolving any difficulties pertaining to their dorms. By changing the status of issues as soon as complaints are resolved, the admin aids in keeping track of the complaints. The Wardon keeps track of the numerous complaints and their progression that students have filed, ensuring the appropriate operation of all such activities.


WHO CAN USE?



WARDEN


Warden can view the complaints filed by the students. They can also check the status of complaints whether they are resolved or not.

USER


The user can submit complaints about the mess, the internet, the electricity, the hygiene, and other hostel-related issues. In order to determine if the problem has been remedied or not, they can also view the status of their complaints.

ADMIN


Admin can view several complaints and pick them to resolve them. After resolving the complaints, the status of the complaints will also be accordingly changed.
